About us

.Série

What is Série?

.Série is a craftsman project meant as service. A more sustainable approach to leather crafting and accessories.
.Série is a service, where you can be a part of a design process. Specializing in the design process. You can learn more about the materials and how to take care of the object in the long run. Ine of our biggest goals is to reduce the waste of marerial and allowing people to think differently about objects and the materials they're made with.

Do you have an idea?

We are questioning the current fashion system. Do we really want to continue to collect and create waste?
We want you to be a part of a design process. We want to show you contemoirary crafting methods, which are more efficient and therefore more affordable and sustainable. You can help us reduce overproduction. We don't have any premade collections and therefore no deadstock. Every piece will be made custom to you and your needs. This way we can ensure a piece you'll love for a lifetime

Laser cutting

.Série is a simplified craftmanship service. We believe that sharing design knowledge can help people understand how more sustainable products can be made. for a better future of fashion and design.
.Série anchors itself in a fab lab (fabrication laboratory) where prototyping is easy with the help of laser cutting machines
Cutting leather by hand is a long process that requires many years of practice to perfect it. Which can make it difficult to acces and share. Industrial methods, such as making molds from metal, can be very expensive and are only used for big productions. That makes it a non viable for personalized products.
